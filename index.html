ABOUT THE MET

The Metropolitan Museum of Art is the largest art museum in the United States and one of the most visited museums in the world. Located in New York City, it houses over two million works of art spanning 5,000 years of history.

Our mission is to collect, preserve, study, and exhibit works of art that represent the highest quality and most significant examples of human creativity.
